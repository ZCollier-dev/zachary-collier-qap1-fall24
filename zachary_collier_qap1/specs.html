Terms and Definitions

While many terms may be self-explanatory, others are often ill-explained.

MSRP - The price at which the manufacturer recommends that retailers sell products at.

GPS - A system which helps track your position on Earth through a satellite. Used to assist with navigation.

hp - The power of the engine, determining how quickly the engine generates torque. 1 Horsepower = 745.699872 Watts of power.

Torque - The rotational force of the engine which translates into acceleration, which in time translates into speed.

Suspension - The system of tires, tire air, shock absorbers and linkages that connects a vehicle to its wheels, allowing motion between components.

Transmission - The system responsibe for transmitting power from the engine to the wheels.

Turbo Compressor - A forced induction device powered by the flow of exhaust gases, used to compress more air into the engine to produce more power.
