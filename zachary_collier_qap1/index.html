Brief Overview

The Ford Bronco 4 Door Outer Banks SUV 2023 is a 4-wheel drive SUV released in Summer 2022, following up its sixth-generation predecessors initially conceptualized in Summer 2020. The Bronco line is a response to the Jeep Wrangler line, competing in the off-road market.

The Bronco, however, failed to gain traction against Jeep during its prior 5 generations. This generation sports a wide variety of versions, both 2 door and 4 door, with some advanced, including, from least to most expensive:

  • Base,
  • Big Bend,
  • Black Diamond,
  • Outer Banks,
  • Badlands,
  • Heritage,
  • Everglades,
  • Wildtrak,
  • Heritage Limited, and
  • Raptor.

The Bronco Outer Banks specifically benefits from solid handling and off-road capabilities. However, the vehicle falls victim to several design flaws affecting reliability on the road, excessive fuel consumption and high prices in an era where money is a notable issue for many.

The Outer Banks 4-Door SUV costs approximately $59,540 Canadian, as reported by driving.ca. Prices may be higher or lower depending on your local dealer.
